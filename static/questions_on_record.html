Questions on the report

To prepapre victims for writing a report, we offer the questions before logging in, giving the victim time to mentally and emotionally prepare. All questions are optional and can be skipped. Most questions are multiple choice options with options to submit longer answers in order to streamline the question process.
  1. What is your name?
  2. What is your email?
  3. Are you unsure that this event would be classified as sexual assault?
  4. If maybe, how did you give consent?
  5. Did you have a previous romantically or sexually-involved relationship with the offender?
  6. What is your relationship with the offender?
  7. Can you identify the suspect?
  8. What does the suspect look like?
  9. What made you feel forced to be with the offender?
  10. What were the events leading up to the assault?
  11. Describe environmental conditions.
  12. What date did the incident occur?
  13. At what time of day?
  14. How many offenders were there?
  15. Where did the assault occur?
  16. If car, do you remember the license, make, model, and year of car? Interior and exterior description?
  17. Where did the you first come into contact with the offender(s)?
  18. Were you awake, asleep, or unconscious?
  19. Was alcohol a factor?
  20. How much did the suspect have to drink?
  21. How much did you drink?
  22. Was the assault facilitated by drugs?
  23. Were you with anyone at the time of the assault? Could this person be a potential witness?
  24. Did you talk to offender before the assault?
  25. What did you say to the offender during the assault?
  26. Did you talk to the offender after the assault?
  27. From what direction did the offender approach you from?
  28. What type of sexual activity was involved?
  29. How did you act physically towards the offender(s)?
  30. What force was used or threatened by the offender(s)?
  31. Were any injuries incurred?
  32. Was medical assistance administered?
  33. If yes to the last question, what hospital administered the medicine? What doctor? When?
  34. How long after the crime are you writing this?
  35. Are you currently medically safe?
  36. Were items taken from your home/ any belongings taken?
  37. Did you talk to an authority about the assault?
  38. What events followed incident?
  39. If anything, what would make you / did make you wait to report this crime?
  40. Any other notes
